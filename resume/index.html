Bio

In high school, Axel activities were many. Axel was part of the honor roll and graduated from Juan Rios Serpa in Ciales, P.R. in 1985. Attended Caguas School of Technology in Bayamon, Puerto Rico. He always had a “thing” for computers. He has made it a point to strive for his goals, which were nothing less than his grandest dreams. As of now, Axel J Resto is a recipient of President List Honor in Seminole State College for the Summer 2018 and 2019 terms. Axel is working towards his Information Systems Technology Bachelor of Science, or A.S. in Digital Media and Television Production. He is taking advantage of DirectConnet to UCF Program. Seminole State partners with the University of Central Florida to offer DirectConnect to UCF, a nationally-recognized program that provides guaranteed entry to UCF.
